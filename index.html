Ultimate HTML Cleaner Setup

Instructions for Users:

Step 1: Make sure your bookmarks bar is visible. If you don't see it below your address bar, press Ctrl+Shift+B (on Windows/Chrome) or Cmd+Shift+B (on Mac).

Step 2: Click and drag the blue button below up to your bookmarks bar and release it.

Save Clean HTML

How to Use It:

1. Navigate to any product page on the McMaster-Carr website.

2. Click the "Save Clean HTML" button you just added to your bookmarks bar.

3. A lightweight, cleaned-up version of the page's HTML will be saved to your Downloads folder.
