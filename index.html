Full Page HTML Saver Setup

Instructions for Users:

Step 1: Make sure your bookmarks bar is visible. If you don't see it below your address bar, press Ctrl+Shift+B (on Windows/Chrome) or Cmd+Shift+B (on Mac).

Step 2: Click and drag the green button below up to your bookmarks bar and release it.

Save HTML (No Scripts)

How to Use It:

1. Navigate to any product page on the McMaster-Carr website.

2. Click the "Save HTML (No Scripts)" button you just added to your bookmarks bar.

3. The complete HTML of the current page (without scripts) will be saved to your Downloads folder.
